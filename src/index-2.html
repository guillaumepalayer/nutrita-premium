Goal driven

Get results by setting goals you can hit consistently. Whether you want to lose fat, control blood sugar, build muscle mass or simply maintain your weight in a healthy manner you can do it with our full support. Sign up to our 7-day free trial to book a free 30min expert consult.

It’s not all about calories

Counting calories isn’t a good approach to fat loss or general health. No one can do it accurately enough long-term, it’s not sustainable, and it doesn’t address root cause issues like hormonal imbalance and metabolic dysfunction. Nutrita Pro tracks your calories but they don’t have to be your focus, as we provide more informative measures of diet quality. For instance, the keto score, dietary insulin load, nutrient completeness, protein-to-energy ratio and more.

Custom recipes

Nutrita applied it’s food scores to hundreds of delicious low-carb recipes created by DitchTheCarbs. Brows over a hundred nutrient dense recipes in line with your carb limit and protein target. Get their keto score, insulin score and nutrient density score to always make the right choice for you.

In-house food scores

Nutrition tracking apps don’t do much except record food and count calories. So Nutrita’s team of scientists and engineers were compelled to do something way, way cooler. They used clinical data, their biochemistry expertise and computer learning to create 3 scores: the insulin score tells you how a food affects the hormone insulin, the keto score tells you what’s keto and what’s not, and the nutrient density score tells you how nourishing a particular food or recipe truly is.
